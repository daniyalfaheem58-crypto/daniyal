FAQ & POLICIES

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) for all orders. This means you only pay when you receive your order. We're working on adding more payment options in the future.
Delivery typically takes 2-3 business days for major cities and 3-5 business days for other areas. We partner with premium courier services to ensure your order arrives safely and on time.
We offer a 7-day return policy for defective or damaged items. The item must be in its original condition with all packaging intact. Please contact our WhatsApp support immediately if you need to initiate a return.
Currently, we only ship within Pakistan. We're exploring options for international shipping in the future. Please follow our social media for updates on this service.
Once your order is shipped, we'll send you the tracking number via WhatsApp. You can use this number to track your package on the courier company's website.
